Life changing story

Since my childhood I have been fond of drawing. For the first time it was just a hobby. Then I realized I want to be a painter and make an great works. When I was sixteen I entered the Minsk College of Arts where I started to open for myself an wonderful world of painting. There I was taught the different ways of drawing, I started to disclose my creativity. When I entered Belarusian State Academy of Arts, my dreams have come: I started to use my drawing skills and my first works in university were really nice. After graduation I worked with Melesta Games as graphic designer. I took part in creating decor and characters for mobile games Toy Defense, Toy Defense 2 and Toy Defense 3. I am always open to exciting job opportunities, feel free to message me.
